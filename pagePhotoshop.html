1. Select the magic wand tool. (edited)
  2. Have your magic wand settings match this:
  3. Click on one color. Anywhere in the picture that has this color will be selected.
  4. Use shift and click on other colors to recolor multiple colors at once. Here I have selected all of the Lucario's blue
  5. Select an adjustment (I will use Hue/Saturation)
  6. Move the sliders around until you get your desired color. Press OK when done.
  7. If you don't like the recolor, you can always use Edit -> Undo (Ctrl + Z). Additionally, you can create an Adjustment layer by clicking on an Adjustment in the Adjustments tab. Don't forget to return to the original layer when moving on to recoloring a new area of the sprite! (edited)
  8. Repeat the steps and you'll be on your way to an amazing (or maybe dreadful) recolor!
